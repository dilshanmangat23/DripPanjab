Our Story

Punjab Drip: Where heritage meets contemporary fashion

Our Mission

Punjab Drip was born from a passion to celebrate Punjabi culture through contemporary fashion. We believe that cultural heritage can be both preserved and evolved through thoughtful, modern interpretations.

Our mission is to create clothing that honors the rich textile traditions, vibrant colors, and artistic expressions of Punjab while appealing to today's global fashion sensibilities.

Through our designs, we aim to help the younger generation connect with their roots and share the beauty of Punjabi culture with the world.

Our Journey

2022

Punjab Drip was founded in Toronto with a small collection of graphic tees featuring Punjabi-inspired designs.

2023

We expanded our product line to include hoodies and accessories, and launched our first full collection: "Phulkari Revival."

2024

Opened our first pop-up store in Vancouver and collaborated with Punjabi music artists for the "Bhangra Beats" collection.

2025

Launched our e-commerce platform to reach customers globally and introduced seasonal collections inspired by different aspects of Punjabi culture.
