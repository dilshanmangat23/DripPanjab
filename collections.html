The Story Behind Our Collections

At Punjab Drip, each collection is a thoughtful blend of Punjabi heritage and contemporary fashion. Our design team researches traditional textile patterns, cultural motifs, and historical garments to create pieces that honor the rich cultural legacy of Punjab.

We work with artisans and cultural experts to ensure our interpretations are authentic while also appealing to modern fashion sensibilities. Our goal is to create clothing that helps the younger generation connect with their cultural roots in a way that feels relevant to their lives today.

From the vibrant colors of Phulkari embroidery to the rhythmic energy of Bhangra dance, each collection tells a story of cultural pride and artistic expression that transcends generations.
