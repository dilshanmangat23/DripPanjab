Product Description

The Bhangra Beats Hoodie is a celebration of Punjabi culture and dance. The design draws inspiration from traditional Bhangra dance movements and the vibrant energy of Punjabi music.

Our designers have studied authentic textile patterns from Punjab and reinterpreted them for modern streetwear. The result is a unique hoodie that stands at the intersection of cultural heritage and contemporary fashion.

The hoodie features:

  • Bhangra-inspired patterns across the chest and sleeves
  • Premium cotton-polyester blend that's both comfortable and durable
  • Double-lined hood with matching drawstrings
  • Spacious kangaroo pocket
  • Ribbed cuffs and hem for a secure fit

Perfect for casual outings, cultural events, or just showing your Punjabi pride on campus or around town.
