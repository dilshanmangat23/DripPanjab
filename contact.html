Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. All payments are securely processed.

What is your shipping policy?

We offer free standard shipping on all orders over $50 within Canada and over $100 for international orders. Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days for domestic orders and 7-14 business days for international orders.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery for items in their original, unworn condition with tags attached. Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer unless the item is defective.

How do I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with tracking information. You can also track your order by logging into your account on our website or contacting our customer service team.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship worldwide! International shipping rates vary by destination and will be calculated at checkout.

How can I collaborate with Punjab Drip?

We're always open to collaborations with artists, designers, and organizations that align with our values. Please contact us with details about your collaboration idea using the form above or email us at collaborations@punjabdrip.com.

Have more questions? Check out our complete FAQ page or contact us directly.
